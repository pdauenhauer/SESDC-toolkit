About Us

Welcome to our project!

Our mission is to help plan for the long-term impact and sustainability of microgrid installations. The main feature of the tool is a customizable simulation that predicts energy production and consumption, as well as socioeconomic impacts of a microgrid system. This web application is designed to help system designers optimize their plans for energy solutions in developing countries.
